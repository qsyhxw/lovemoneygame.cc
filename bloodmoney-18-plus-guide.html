Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! BloodMoney contains zero sexual content. It's purely a horror/violence game. However, if you're sensitive to graphic violence, blood, or psychological horror themes, be aware that BloodMoney does contain those elements.

Why do people ask if BloodMoney is a "fetish game"? ‚ñº

This confusion likely stems from LoveMoney, the fan-made sequel with explicit sexual content. Some players mix up the two games. BloodMoney itself is not a fetish game ‚Äî it's a horror game about violence and moral choices.

Is there an uncensored patch for BloodMoney? ‚ñº

No. BloodMoney doesn't need an uncensor patch because there's no censored content to begin with. The game on itch.io is the complete, full version. There is no sexual content to "uncensor."

Is BloodMoney related to LoveMoney? ‚ñº

Yes and no. BloodMoney is the original game by SHROOMYCHRIST Studios. LoveMoney is an unofficial fan-made sequel by Buwu that takes the same premise (earn $25,000 with Harvey) but replaces violence with sexual content. What is BloodMoney 2 / Human Expenditure Program? ‚ñº

BloodMoney 2 (also called "Human Expenditure Program") is the official sequel by SHROOMYCHRIST Studios, released in September 2025. Unlike LoveMoney (the fan sequel), BloodMoney 2 is an authorized continuation by the original developer.

Can minors play BloodMoney? ‚ñº

No. While BloodMoney doesn't have a formal age gate on itch.io, the game's violent content, gore, and psychological horror themes make it inappropriate for minors. It is intended for players 18 and older.
