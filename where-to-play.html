Where to Play LoveMoney — FAQ

Common questions about platforms and availability

Where can I play LoveMoney game? ▼

You can play LoveMoney in three main ways: 1) Free in your web browser at our Play Online page, 2) Download for Windows or macOS from the official itch.io page, or 3) Play on mobile browsers (Android/iOS). All options are completely free.

Is LoveMoney on Steam? ▼

No. LoveMoney is not available on Steam. The game is exclusively distributed through itch.io by developer Buwu. There are no plans to release on Steam at this time.

Is LoveMoney completely free? ▼

Yes, 100% free. LoveMoney has no paywalls, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Both the browser version and the itch.io download are free. The in-game currency ($25,000 goal) is part of the story, not a real transaction.

Can I play LoveMoney on my phone? ▼

Yes! You can play in your phone's browser (both Android and iOS). For Android, unofficial APKs exist but are not from the official developer. Desktop play is recommended for the best visual novel experience. See our Mobile Guide for details.

Which version should I download — OLD or UPDATE? ▼

Download the UPDATE version. Released in September 2025, it includes additional mechanics, secret content, and a new story. The OLD version contains bugs and unfinished features.

Is LoveMoney appropriate for all ages? ▼

No. LoveMoney is rated 18+ and contains mature content including sexual themes, moral dilemmas, and potentially disturbing imagery. It is intended exclusively for adult audiences. See our 18+ Content Guide for details.
