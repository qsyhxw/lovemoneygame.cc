Scene Guide FAQ

Can I skip explicit scenes?

The best way to avoid explicit content is to never purchase the Soap item. If you avoid Soap entirely, you'll stay on the Friendship path which has no explicit content. Once Soap is purchased, there's no way to skip scenes in the current game version.

How long does each phase take?

Introduction: 5-10 minutes. Shop Phase: 15-30 minutes depending on click speed. Intimacy Phase: 10-20 minutes if triggered. Ending: 5-10 minutes. Total: 30-60 minutes per playthrough.

What's the safest path for sensitive players?

For the mildest experience: 1) Buy the Feather for efficiency, 2) Avoid purchasing Soap or other late-game items, 3) Click to $25,000. This leads to the Friendship ending with minimal suggestive content.

Do I need to see all scenes for 100% completion?

There's no achievement or completion tracker in LoveMoney. You can enjoy whichever paths appeal to you. The UPDATE version's 12 notes provide additional lore but aren't required to "complete" the game.

What's the desktop wallpaper scene?

On desktop versions, certain endings (particularly bad endings and secret ending) may change your computer's wallpaper to an unsettling image of Harvey. This is intentional, not malware. Players have reported being surprised by this when closing the game.
