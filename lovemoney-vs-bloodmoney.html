The Fundamental Difference

While both games share the same $25,000 goal and Harvey Harvington character, they explore completely opposite moral dilemmas. BLOODMONEY asks "How far will you go with violence for survival?" while LoveMoney asks "How far will you push intimate boundaries for money?" This thematic shift transforms the entire gameplay experience despite similar clicker mechanics.
