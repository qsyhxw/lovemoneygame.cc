The Story Behind LoveMoney

In LoveMoney, you play as a protagonist facing a life-threatening medical crisis. You need $25,000 to pay for an urgent, life-saving operation — money far beyond your means. Leaving the hospital in despair, you stumble upon a small booth where you meet Harvey Harvington, a charming and enigmatic figure who becomes both your solution and your greatest moral test.

Harvey offers you a way to earn the money you need — through increasingly intimate interactions. What starts as simple clicking to earn cash quickly evolves into a complex web of moral dilemmas, romantic entanglements, and psychological tension. The game constantly asks: how far will you go to survive?

LoveMoney reimagines the premise of BloodMoney (by SHROOMYCHRIST), swapping pain and violence for intimacy and emotional complexity. While BloodMoney had players inflict harm on Harvey to earn money, LoveMoney explores what happens when love — not pain — becomes the currency.

How Does LoveMoney Work?

Core mechanics explained

👆 Clicker Mechanics

At its core, LoveMoney is a clicker game. You interact with Harvey by clicking, starting with basic interactions that earn small amounts of money. As you accumulate funds, you can unlock increasingly intimate tools and interactions through the in-game shop, each boosting your earnings per click.

📖 Visual Novel Narrative

Layered on top of the clicking is a rich choice-driven narrative. Harvey's reactions and dialogue change dynamically based on your choices. Your decisions affect affection points, unlock story branches, and ultimately determine which of the game's multiple endings you'll reach.

⚖️ Moral Choice System

Every purchase and interaction carries moral weight. You can rush toward the $25,000 goal through aggressive means, or take a slower, more ethical path. The game tracks your choices and judges your moral compass, leading to different story outcomes. See our Moral Choices Guide for details.

🏆 Multiple Endings

LoveMoney features multiple endings that depend on the moral and romantic choices you make throughout the game. Community sources report varying numbers of endings, with several main routes independently verified. Use our Ending Calculator to predict your route.

🛒 Item Shop & Resource Management

The in-game shop lets you spend earned money on various items and upgrades that change how you interact with Harvey. Each purchase affects both your earning speed and your relationship, creating constant tension between financial goals and emotional consequences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is LoveMoney game? ▼

LoveMoney is a free fan-made visual novel and clicker RPG by developer Buwu, inspired by BloodMoney. You interact with Harvey Harvington to earn money for a life-saving operation through clicking mechanics and moral choices that lead to multiple endings. It's available on web browsers, Windows, macOS, and Android.

Is LoveMoney free to play? ▼

Yes, completely free. You can play the full game online in your browser or download it from Buwu's itch.io page at no cost. There are no in-app purchases or hidden fees.

Who made LoveMoney? ▼

LoveMoney was created by Buwu, an independent game developer. It's a fan-made sequel to BloodMoney, which was originally created by SHROOMYCHRIST. Buwu is also working on a sequel called "Love Together."

Is LoveMoney appropriate for children? ▼

No. LoveMoney is rated 18+ and contains mature content including sexual themes, moral dilemmas, blood, and potentially disturbing imagery. It is intended exclusively for adult audiences. See our 18+ Content Guide.

How many endings does LoveMoney have? ▼

LoveMoney features multiple endings based on your moral and romantic choices. While the exact number is debated by the community, several main routes have been verified. Use our Ending Calculator to predict your route.
