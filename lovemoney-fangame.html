What is a Fangame?

A fangame is a video game created by fans rather than the original developer or publisher. These games are typically unofficial, non-commercial projects made out of passion for an existing game, series, or genre. Fangames can range from tributes and remakes to entirely new stories inspired by the original work's themes or mechanics.

In the visual novel community, fangames often explore alternative storylines, character perspectives, or thematic variations of popular titles. They're usually distributed for free on platforms like itch.io and represent the creativity and dedication of independent developers who want to contribute to the gaming community.

Lovemoney falls into this category as a fan-made visual novel by developer Buwu. It's not an official sequel, expansion, or licensed product ‚Äî it's an independent creative work shared freely with players who enjoy this genre of games.
