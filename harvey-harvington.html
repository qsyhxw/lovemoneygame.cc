Who Is Harvey Harvington?

Harvey Harvington is the central character in the psychological horror visual novel BLOODMONEY! and its sequel HUMAN EXPENDITURE PROGRAM, both created by indie developer SHROOMYCHRIST. He also appears in LoveMoney, a fan-made 18+ visual novel by developer Buwu.

In BLOODMONEY!, Harvey appears as a vendor who helps players earn money for an expensive medical treatment. The core gameplay involves clicking on Harvey to earn in-game currency, with the amount increasing as players inflict more pain using various tools. This mechanic explores themes of compassion, morality, and the lengths people will go for money.

Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Harvey Harvington?

Harvey Harvington is 32 years old, with his birthday on June 27th according to official BLOODMONEY! lore.

Is Harvey Harvington married? Who is his wife?

Yes, Harvey is married to Eun-Mi Harvington (age 34, also known as Joy or Choi Eun-Mi). They have a son named Toby. Learn more on the Eun-Mi character page.

Is Harvey Harvington a boy or girl?

Harvey Harvington is male. He is a 32-year-old married man with a wife and son.

Does Eun-Mi hate Harvey?

No, Eun-Mi does not hate Harvey. They are married and have a son together. In BLOODMONEY!, Eun-Mi appears as a supportive wife. In HEP (HUMAN EXPENDITURE PROGRAM), players can actually control Eun-Mi during certain segments.

Can Harvey Harvington die?

Yes. In BLOODMONEY!'s Bad Ending, Harvey dies if the player purchases and uses the gun. In LoveMoney, Harvey's fate varies depending on your choices and the ending you get.

Is there 18+ content with Harvey Harvington?

Yes, LoveMoney by Buwu is an 18+ fan game featuring Harvey with explicit adult content. The content is triggered by purchasing the Soap item. BLOODMONEY! focuses on horror/violence rather than sexual content. See the 18+ Guide for details.

Is Harvey Harvington British?

Harvey's exact nationality is not officially confirmed. His wife Eun-Mi is Korean-American. Based on his mannerisms and style, fans speculate he may be American or British.

Who created Harvey Harvington?

Harvey was created by SHROOMYCHRIST for BLOODMONEY! (August 2025). Developer Buwu later created LoveMoney as a fan game featuring Harvey. See the Buwu developer page.
