Who Is Harvey Harvington?

Harvey Harvington is the central character in the psychological horror visual novel BLOODMONEY! and its sequel HUMAN EXPENDITURE PROGRAM, both created by indie developer SHROOMYCHRIST. He also appears in LoveMoney, a fan-made 18+ visual novel by developer Buwu.

In BLOODMONEY!, Harvey appears as a vendor who helps players earn money for an expensive medical treatment. The core gameplay involves clicking on Harvey to earn in-game currency, with the amount increasing as players inflict more pain using various tools. This mechanic explores themes of compassion, morality, and the lengths people will go for money.
