Harvey Ending Guide

What is The Awkward Acquaintance?

The Awkward Acquaintance is a Compromise ending - neither fully good nor bad. You successfully earn the money for your operation, but Harvey is visibly uncomfortable with your constant intimate advances like cheek kisses. The relationship ends on an uncertain, awkward note.

This ending sits between The Savior (best ending) and the bad endings. You push boundaries but stop before crossing the line into truly harmful territory.

FAQ

Is this a good or bad ending?

It's a neutral/compromise ending. You get the money but the relationship with Harvey is awkward and uncertain.

What makes Harvey flustered?

Constant intimate actions like cheek kisses combined with purchasing feather, brush, and hair wreath creates the flustered reaction.

Can I get a better ending?

Yes. Follow the True Ending guide to get The Savior ending where Harvey is comfortable.

What happens if I buy lipstick?

You'll get a bad ending instead. Lipstick crosses into "Point of No Return" territory.
