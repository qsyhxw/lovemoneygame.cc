How to Get The Savior Ending

The Savior is the true ending and best good ending in LoveMoney. This guide shows you exactly how to reach $25,000 while maintaining Harvey's dignity and respect.

View Walkthrough See All Endings

Quick Summary

  • ✅ Reach $25,000 through clicking and upgrades
  • ✅ Buy safe items: feather, brush, wreath (optional)
  • ❌ DO NOT buy lipstick
  • ❌ DO NOT buy soap (final item)
  • ✅ Result: Harvey congratulates you, you get your operation

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Follow these steps carefully to unlock The Savior ending on your first playthrough.

1

Start the Game

Begin playing LoveMoney. Your goal is to earn $25,000 for a life-saving operation by interacting with Harvey.

2

Click to Earn Money

Click on Harvey to earn money. Each click generates income that accumulates toward your $25,000 goal.

3

Purchase Safe Items Only

As you earn money, items become available in the shop. You may purchase:

  • ✅ Feather - Safe to purchase
  • ✅ Brush - Safe to purchase
  • ✅ Wreath - Safe to purchase

These items increase your money per click without crossing major boundaries.

4

Avoid Intimate Items

Critical: Do NOT purchase these items:

  • ❌ Lipstick - Crosses first major boundary
  • ❌ Soap - Final item, triggers worst ending

⚠️ Warning: Purchasing lipstick or soap will lock you out of The Savior ending and lead to bad endings instead.

5

Reach $25,000

Continue clicking and using your purchased upgrades until you reach the $25,000 goal. Monitor your progress in the game interface.

6

Unlock The Savior Ending

Once you reach $25,000 without purchasing lipstick or soap, you'll unlock The Savior ending. Harvey congratulates you on your success, and you receive your operation without causing him harm or distress.

✓ This ending emphasizes moral restraint and financial success without compromising Harvey's dignity.

Tips for Success

💡 Focus on Clicking Efficiency

The feather, brush, and wreath increase your money per click. Purchase them early to speed up progress.

💡 Resist Temptation

When lipstick and soap become available, ignore them completely. They offer higher earnings but lock you into bad endings.

💡 Track Your Progress

Keep an eye on your total earnings. The Savior ending requires patience but is achievable with safe items only.

FAQ

Can I buy any items and still get The Savior?

Yes. You can purchase feather, brush, and wreath. Just avoid lipstick and soap.

What happens if I accidentally buy lipstick?

You'll be locked into a bad ending path. You'll need to restart the game to get The Savior ending.

Is The Savior the only good ending?

The Savior is the best good ending. There are other Pure Heart endings, but this is considered the true ending. See all endings guide.

How long does it take to reach $25,000?

With safe items only, expect 30-45 minutes of clicking. Using feather, brush, and wreath speeds this up significantly.
