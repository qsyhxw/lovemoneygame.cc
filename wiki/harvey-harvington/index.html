Who is Harvey Harvington?

Harvey Harvington is the named figure at the center of BLOODMONEY!, where the official project page introduces him as the man in the roadside stall who offers money in exchange for clicks and escalating harm. In the sequel HUMAN EXPENDITURE PROGRAM, the official project page again centers the experience around owning and controlling Harvey.

For LoveMoney players, Harvey matters because Buwu's public itch.io creator profile describes LoveMoney as a BloodMoney fangame to love Harvey. That phrasing directly connects LoveMoney search intent back to the Harvey character, even though the strongest official primary canon still lives in the SHROOMYCHRIST releases.

Verified series appearances

BLOODMONEY!

The official itch.io page identifies Harvey Harvington as the character in the roadside stall and frames the game's moral loop around how much harm the player is willing to inflict on him to reach the $25,000 goal.

HUMAN EXPENDITURE PROGRAM

The official sequel page again names Harvey directly and describes the program as a system where the player has control over Harvey and his life, making him the recurring anchor of the series rather than a one-off side character.

LoveMoney

Buwu's public itch.io creator profile positions LoveMoney as a fangame built around loving Harvey. That is enough to treat LoveMoney as a meaningful secondary context for Harvey searches, while still keeping this entity page grounded in the BLOODMONEY canon first.

What can be stated carefully about his lore

Community-documented references and creator-answer archives consistently present Harvey as a married adult character with close ties to Eun-Mi. However, the storefront pages themselves are lighter on those details than on the core game premise.

For that reason, this main wiki page keeps the harder claims modest: Harvey is central to the BLOODMONEY universe, he recurs in the sequel, and his family context is important enough that Buwu explicitly said Harvey's wife-and-child canon would remain part of LoveMoney's setup.
