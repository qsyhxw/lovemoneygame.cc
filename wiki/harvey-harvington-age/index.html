How old is Harvey in Stardew Valley?

Harvey's exact age is not stated in Stardew Valley. The game does not provide specific ages for any of the marriage candidates.

Based on his profession as a doctor and dialogue references to attending medical school, the community estimates Harvey to be in his mid-30s to early 40s (approximately 32-40 years old). This is player speculation based on the education and training required for his profession, not official information.

Harvey is described in community discussions as "a little old for a bachelor" and is generally considered one of the older marriage candidates in the game.

When is Harvey's birthday?

Harvey's birthday is Winter 14. This is confirmed information from the game and appears consistently across official sources including the Stardew Valley Wiki and gaming guides.

You can give Harvey gifts on his birthday to receive 8x friendship points instead of the normal amount, making it an important date for players pursuing a relationship with him.

What is Harvey's profession and background?

Harvey is the doctor in Pelican Town who runs the medical clinic. He lives in a small apartment above the clinic and is deeply committed to the health of the townsfolk.

According to game dialogue, Harvey originally wanted to be a pilot but changed career paths due to myopia (nearsightedness) and a fear of heights. He is described as caring, reserved, and friendly in temperament.

Harvey is not originally from Stardew Valley and has no mentioned family members in the game. He lives alone and is one of the twelve marriage candidates available to players.

Does Harvey have a specified height or family?

Height: Stardew Valley does not provide official height measurements for any characters. Any height claims found online are fan speculation.

Family: Harvey has no mentioned family members in the game. Unlike some other characters who have parents or siblings in Pelican Town, Harvey appears to live independently with no family connections revealed in dialogue or events.
