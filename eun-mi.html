Eun-Mi Harvington - Harvey's Wife Wiki & Character Guide

Eun-Mi Harvington (also known as Joy or Choi Eun-Mi) is Harvey Harvington's wife in the BLOODMONEY! universe. This guide covers her age, background, role in the games, relationship with Harvey, and complete character lore.

Who Is Eun-Mi Harvington?

Eun-Mi Harvington is a central character in the BLOODMONEY! universe created by SHROOMYCHRIST. While she doesn't appear directly in the original BLOODMONEY! game, she is frequently referenced in the lore and plays a crucial role in understanding Harvey's backstory and motivations.

In the sequel game Human Expenditure Program (HEP), Eun-Mi becomes a playable character. Players take on her perspective as she exerts control over Harvey, providing a dark look into their troubled marriage and the psychological dynamics at play.

Her character represents the complex themes of the BLOODMONEY! universe: obsession with money, deteriorating relationships, and the moral compromises people make when facing financial hardship.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Eun-Mi Harvington?

Eun-Mi (also called Joy or Choi Eun-Mi) is Harvey Harvington's wife. She is 34 years old, Korean-American, and a key character in the BLOODMONEY! lore.

How old is Eun-Mi?

Eun-Mi is 34 years old, two years older than her husband Harvey (32). Her birthday is November 4th.

Is Eun-Mi playable in any game?

Yes! Eun-Mi is the playable protagonist in Human Expenditure Program (HEP), the sequel to BLOODMONEY! developed by SHROOMYCHRIST.

Why is Eun-Mi considered a villain?

The lore implies Eun-Mi created the "program" that traps Harvey. After Harvey lost his job, she developed an obsession with money. However, her true motivations remain somewhat ambiguous.

Does Eun-Mi appear in LoveMoney?

Eun-Mi does not appear directly in LoveMoney (the fan game by Buwu). However, she is referenced when Harvey expresses guilt about betraying his wife in the bad endings.
