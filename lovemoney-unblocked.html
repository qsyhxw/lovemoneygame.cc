What Is LoveMoney?

LoveMoney is a fan-made visual novel and clicker RPG created by independent developer Buwu, inspired by the original BloodMoney by SHROOMYCHRIST. The game centers on Harvey Harvington, a character you interact with to earn $25,000 for a life-saving operation.

The game features choice-driven gameplay with moral dilemmas and multiple endings based on your decisions. Originally released in 2025 by Buwu on itch.io, the game is available for Windows and macOS download, and can also be played directly in web browsers.
